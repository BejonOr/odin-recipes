Mashed Potatoes

Description

A recipe to make rice. A very simple dish that can be made by anyone.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds baking potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled, or to taste (Optional)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  3. Add potatoes and garlic
  4. Simmer until potatoes are tender, 15 to 20 minutes
  5. heat milk and butter in a small saucepan over low heat until butter is melted
  6. Drain potatoes and return to the pot
  7. Slowly add warm milk mixture, blending it with mixer until potatoes are smooth and creamy
  8. Season with salt and pepper
  9. Enjoy
