Rice

Description

A recipe to make rice. A very simple dish that can be made by anyone.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 2 cups white rice
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder(optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups boiling water

Directions

  1. Heat oil in large saucepan over medium heat
  2. Add rice, garlic powder and salt
  3. Pour in boiling water to cover rice by about 1 inch
  4. Bring to a boil
  5. Reduce heat to low
  6. Cover pan
  7. Simmer until water is absorbed and rice is tender, 20 to 30 minutes
